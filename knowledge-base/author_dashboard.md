# Author Dashboard Guide

## Overview

Your BookLeaf Author Dashboard is your command center for managing your publishing journey. Access real-time sales data, track manuscript progress, communicate with your team, and manage your author profile – all from one convenient location.

**Access**: Log in at [bookleaf.com/authors](https://bookleaf.com/authors) using your email and password.

## Getting Started

### First-Time Login

When you first access your dashboard:

1. **Complete Your Profile**: Add your bio, photo, social media links, and website
2. **Set Payment Preferences**: Configure how you'd like to receive royalty payments
3. **Review Your Agreement**: Digital copy of your publishing contract
4. **Take the Tour**: Interactive walkthrough of key features (optional but recommended)

### Dashboard Navigation

The main navigation menu includes:
- **Home**: Overview and quick stats
- **My Books**: Manuscript status and book management
- **Sales & Analytics**: Detailed sales reports and trends
- **Royalties**: Payment history and statements
- **Marketing**: Promotional tools and campaigns
- **Messages**: Communication with your BookLeaf team
- **Settings**: Account preferences and configuration

## My Books Section

### Book Management

For each of your titles:

**Manuscript Status Tracker**:
- Visual timeline showing current stage
- Estimated completion date for each phase
- Assigned team members (editor, designer, coordinator)
- Pending approval items (cover designs, edited chapters)

**Book Details**:
- View and edit metadata (description, categories, keywords)
- Upload updated manuscripts (before final approval)
- Preview cover designs and interior layouts
- Manage pricing across formats and regions

**Files and Assets**:
- Download high-resolution cover images
- Access formatted manuscript files
- Marketing materials (book images, author photos, press releases)
- Style guide and brand assets

### Manuscript Submission

**Submit New Work**:
1. Click "Submit New Manuscript"
2. Upload file (DOCX, PDF, or EPUB)
3. Complete metadata form:
   - Title and subtitle
   - Genre and sub-genre
   - Target audience and age range
   - Word count
   - Synopsis (150-300 words)
   - Keywords (5-10 relevant terms)
4. Add optional materials (cover letter, marketing plan)
5. Review and submit

**Track Submissions**:
- See status of all submitted works
- View reader feedback and editor notes
- Receive decision notifications
- Access rejection feedback (if applicable)

**Configure Payment Method**:
- **Direct Deposit**: Add bank account (routing and account number)
- **PayPal**: Link your PayPal account

- **Wire Transfer**: For international authors (bank details required)

- Change payment frequency (if eligible for monthly payments)

### Royalty Statements

**Statement Details**:
Each quarterly statement includes:
- Opening balance (carryover from previous quarter)

- Gross earnings calculation

- Net payment amount
- Tax withholding (if applicable)

**Understanding Your Statement**:
- Interactive tooltips explain each line item
- Download PDF for your records

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Can't Log In**:
- Use "Forgot Password" link to reset
- Check email spam folder for reset link
- Ensure caps lock is off
- Try different browser or clear cache
- Contact support if issues persist

**Sales Not Updating**:
- Sales data refreshes daily at noon ET
- Some platforms report with 24-48 hour delay
- Check "Last Updated" timestamp on sales dashboard
- Refresh browser page

**Payment Not Received**:
- Verify you've met $25 minimum threshold
- Check payment method is correctly configured
- Review spam folder for payment notification email
- Payments process within 5 business days of quarter end
- Contact royalties@bookleaf.com if payment is more than 7 days late

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How often are sales updated?**
A: Sales data is updated daily by noon ET with the previous day's activity.

**Q: Can I see individual buyer information?**
A: No, for privacy reasons, we don't share customer personal information.

**Q: Can multiple people access my dashboard?**
A: You can grant limited access to assistants or co-authors through Settings → User Management.

**Q: How do I download all my sales data?**
A: Go to Sales & Analytics → Reports, select "All Time" date range, and download CSV.

**Q: What if I spot an error in my sales data?**
A: Use the "Report Issue" button on the Sales dashboard or contact support immediately.
