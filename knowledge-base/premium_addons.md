## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I add services after my book is already published?**
A: Yes! Marketing, distribution, and platform-building services can be added anytime. Some production services (like cover redesigns) can be done as updates to existing books.

**Q: Do add-ons delay my publication timeline?**
A: Some services may extend your timeline (noted in service description). We'll provide updated timeline when you add services. Marketing services typically don't delay publication.

**Q: Can I get a custom quote for services not listed?**
A: Absolutely! Contact our premium services team at premium@bookleaf.com to discuss custom needs.

**Q: Are these services refundable?**
A: Due to the custom nature of these services, refunds are limited. We offer satisfaction guarantees with revision rights. See full terms in service agreement.

**Q: Can I use my own vendors for some services?**
A: Yes, you can mix BookLeaf and external services. For example, you could use your own cover designer while purchasing our marketing package.

**Q: Do authors typically purchase add-ons?**
A: About 60% of BookLeaf authors purchase at least one add-on service. The most popular are audiobook production, premium marketing, and author websites.

**Q: How do I know which services are right for me?**
A: Schedule a free 30-minute consultation with our premium services advisor. We'll review your goals, budget, and book to recommend the best fit.
