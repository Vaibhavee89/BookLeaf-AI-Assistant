# BookLeaf Royalty Structure and Payment Terms

## Overview

At BookLeaf Publishing, we believe in fair and transparent compensation for our authors. Our royalty structure is designed to reward your creative work while allowing us to maintain the high-quality services you expect.

## Royalty Rates

### Print Books

**Standard Trade Paperback**:
- Retail sales: **40% of net receipts**
- Wholesale sales: **35% of net receipts**
- Bulk orders (50+ copies): **30% of net receipts**

**Hardcover Editions**:
- Retail sales: **45% of net receipts**
- Wholesale sales: **40% of net receipts**

**Definition of Net Receipts**: The amount BookLeaf receives after retailer/distributor discounts and before production costs. Typically 50-60% of cover price for print books.

**Example Calculation** (Trade Paperback):
- Cover price: $16.99
- Retailer discount (40%): -$6.80
- Net receipt: $10.19
- Author royalty (40%): **$4.08 per book**

### Digital Books (eBooks)

**All Digital Formats**:
- Direct sales (BookLeaf.com): **70% of list price**
- Third-party platforms (Amazon, Apple, etc.): **60% of net receipts**
- Subscription services (Kindle Unlimited, Scribd): **55% of net receipts**

**Example Calculation** (eBook):
- List price: $9.99
- Amazon sale (70% to BookLeaf): $6.99
- Author royalty (60% of net): **$4.19 per book**

### Audiobooks

**Audiobook Royalties**:
- Direct sales: **50% of net receipts**
- Audible/platform sales: **45% of net receipts**
- Subscription listens: **40% of net receipts**

**Note**: Audiobook production costs are typically shared 50/50 between author and publisher, or you may arrange your own production.

## Payment Schedule

### Standard Payment Terms

**Frequency**: Royalties are paid **quarterly**:
- Q1 (Jan-Mar): Paid by April 30th
- Q2 (Apr-Jun): Paid by July 31st
- Q3 (Jul-Sep): Paid by October 31st
- Q4 (Oct-Dec): Paid by January 31st

**Minimum Payment Threshold**: $25.00
- If your royalties don't reach $25 in a quarter, they roll over to the next quarter
- No maximum threshold – we pay all earned royalties

**Payment Method**:
- Direct deposit (preferred – setup in your dashboard)
- PayPal
- Physical check (additional $5 processing fee)
- International wire transfer (fees may apply)

### Accelerated Payment Option

For established authors with consistent sales:
- **Monthly payments** available when average quarterly earnings exceed $500
- Payment within 30 days of month-end
- Request through your author dashboard under "Payment Settings"

## Returns and Adjustments

### Returns Policy

**Print Books**:
- Retailers can return unsold print books within 12 months
- Returns are deducted from future royalty payments
- Typical return rate: 5-15% (varies by genre and season)

**Digital Books**:
- Platform-specific refund policies (usually 7-14 days)
- Refunded sales are deducted from next payment
- Digital return rate typically under 5%

### Royalty Adjustments

Reasons for adjustments:
- Returns and refunds
- Pricing promotions (your approval required for royalty-impacting promotions)
- Currency exchange fluctuations (international sales)
- Accounting corrections or errors

All adjustments are detailed in your royalty statement with full explanations.

## Special Circumstances

### Promotional Pricing

**Author-Initiated Promotions**:
- You can schedule temporary price reductions in your dashboard
- Royalty percentage remains the same (based on reduced price)
- Recommended for new releases, holidays, or marketing campaigns

**BookLeaf-Initiated Promotions**:
- We may request to include your book in site-wide promotions
- Requires your approval if discount exceeds 25%
- Often results in volume increases that offset lower per-unit royalties

### Subsidiary Rights

**Rights We Help License** (we share net proceeds 50/50):
- Foreign language translations
- Film/TV adaptation rights
- Large print editions
- Book club editions
- Excerpt/anthology inclusions

**Rights You Retain** (100% to you):
- Merchandising rights
- Character licensing
- Sequel and prequel rights in other formats
- Public speaking and appearance fees

## International Sales

### Currency and Conversion

- International sales reported in USD
- Conversion rate: Daily rate at time of sale
- International royalties subject to local withholding taxes (where applicable)
- Tax documentation: W-8BEN or W-9 required

### International Territories

We distribute to:
- United States and Canada
- United Kingdom and European Union
- Australia and New Zealand
- Select markets in Asia and Latin America

Royalty rates are consistent across territories unless noted in your agreement.

## Tax Considerations

### Tax Documents

**U.S. Authors**:
- Receive 1099-MISC by January 31st (if earnings > $600/year)
- Royalties considered self-employment income
- Quarterly estimated tax payments recommended for high earners

**International Authors**:
- Receive 1099-NEC with withholding details
- May need to claim foreign tax credit in home country
- Consult with tax professional regarding treaty benefits

**Important**: BookLeaf does not provide tax advice. Please consult a qualified tax professional.

## Auditing Rights

You have the right to audit BookLeaf's records:
- Must provide 60 days written notice
- Audit at your expense unless discrepancy > 5%
- Conducted by certified public accountant
- Access to relevant sales records for your titles only

## Advance Against Royalties

### Advance Structure

While not standard for all authors, advances may be offered to:
- Previously published authors with proven sales record
- Books with significant pre-publication buzz or platform
- Authors represented by literary agents

**Typical Advance Ranges**:
- Debut authors: $1,000 - $5,000
- Mid-list authors: $5,000 - $15,000
- Established authors: $15,000 - $50,000+

**Repayment Terms**:
- Advances are recouped from future royalties
- No repayment required if book doesn't earn out
- Paid in 2-3 installments: signing, manuscript delivery, publication

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: When will I receive my first payment?**
A: After publication, sales are reported with a 60-90 day lag (retailer reporting delay). Expect first payment approximately 4-6 months after publication.

**Q: How do I track my earnings?**
A: Log in to your author dashboard at bookleaf.com/authors. Your dashboard updates daily with the previous day's sales.

**Q: What if I don't agree with my royalty statement?**
A: Contact author support at royalties@bookleaf.com within 90 days of statement date. We'll investigate and resolve any discrepancies.

**Q: Can I get an advance on future royalties?**
A: For established authors with consistent sales, we offer advance programs. Contact your publishing coordinator to discuss eligibility.

**Q: Do I pay for returns?**
A: No, you're not charged. Returns simply reduce future royalty payments. You're only paid for net sales.

**Q: How are bundled or discounted sales calculated?**
A: Royalties are based on actual net receipts from the sale, not the original list price.
